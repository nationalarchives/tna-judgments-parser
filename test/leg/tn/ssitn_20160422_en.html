Transposition Note for Directive (EU) 2015/2203 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 25 November 2015 on the approximation of the laws of the Member States relating to caseins and caseinates intended for human consumption (the “Directive”).

This Transposition Note sets out how the Directive is transposed in Scotland by the Caseins and Caseinates (Scotland) (No. 2) Regulations 2016 which revoke and replace The Caseins and Caseinates (Scotland) Regulations 1986 (as amended).
